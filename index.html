Frequently Asked Questions

What is HOABL Goa? +

HOABL Goa refers to premium real estate developments by The House of Abhinandan Lodha (HOABL), including sea-facing plots in South Goa (Gulf of Goa) and an island township in North Goa (One Goa).

Are these projects RERA registered? +

Yes, both projects are fully RERA approved. One Goa (Bicholim): PRGO08231988 | Gulf of Goa (Vasco): PRGO01242125. This ensures 100% transparency.

What are the starting prices? +

Prices start from ₹1.04 Cr* for plots in North Goa and ₹2.88 Cr* for sea-facing plots in South Goa. Detailed pricing for villas and apartments is available on request.

Can NRIs invest in HOABL Goa? +

Absolutely. Branded land in Goa is a preferred asset class for NRIs. We offer remote booking facilities and virtual tours for international buyers.

What is the benefit of 'Branded Land'? +

Branded land by HOABL provides security, high-end amenities, and professional management—things that unorganized local plots often lack.

Who will manage the maintenance? +

HOABL ensures institutional-grade maintenance of roads, security, and clubhouses, protecting your asset's long-term value.

How far is MOPA Airport? +

One Goa (North Goa) is approx 45 mins away, while Gulf of Goa is closer to Dabolim Airport (approx 15-20 mins).

What are the registration costs? +

Costs vary by property value. Our consultant can provide a precise cost sheet including stamp duty and registration during your call.

Is there a booking facility available? +

Yes, we offer digital booking for outstation and international clients to ensure you don't miss out on prime inventory.
