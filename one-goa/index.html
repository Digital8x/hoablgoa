Common Enquiries

One Goa FAQs

Answers to the most common questions about the Ibiza-inspired island township in Bicholim.

What is the 'Man-Made Sea' at One Goa?

One Goa features a unique, engineered water body that mimics a sea experience, complete with beach vibes and crystal clear water, exclusively for residents.

What property types are available?

One Goa offers both residential plots (starting from 1,539 sq.ft) and luxury apartments (starting from 920 sq.ft) within a 100+ acre gated township.

Who is Miros Hotels?

Miros Hotels is the 5-star hospitality partner managing the clubhouses and overall resort-style experience at One Goa to ensure international standards.

What is the investment potential of Bicholim?

Bicholim is North Goa's rising star. With the MOPA airport and large-scale institutional developments like One Goa, property values are projected to grow significantly as infrastructure completes.
